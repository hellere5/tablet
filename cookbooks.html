Cookbooks

Here you will find all the different cookbooks I love to reference when cooking!

This is by far one of my favorite cookbooks, as you can tell from my recipe page. Tieghan Gerard has inspired me, through her cookbooks and Instagram posts over the past few years. Her recipes are so much fun and are always so tasty.

Adeena Sussman is a famous chef who lives in Tel Aviv. Her Sababa cookbook was named one of the best fall 2019 cookbooks by The New York Times, Bon Appétit, and Food & Wine and I completely understand why. This cookbook is filled with plenty of amazing recipes. I definitely recommend you buy this cookbook and make some of her wonderful meals.

Bon Appétit is another recipe book that I recommend you check out. Their recipes are like no other - you can find almost anything in this cookbook and will be happy to have made it. The book is very detailed to ensure that you won't mess up and everything will taste wonderful.

I can never get enough of Half Baked Harvest! Her recipes are just so good. Check out this other cookbook of hers to make some simple but tasty recipes. You can find anything from breakfast to lunch to dessert to even snacks in this perfect cookbook.
