Eliza Heller

My name is Eliza Heller and I am a junior at the University of Michigan working to receive my BSI from the School of Information and a minor from the Ross School of Business. I have always been interested in cooking and eating culturally diverse foods and wanted to share my favorite recipes with you!

I am from Cleveland, Ohio and have lived there my whole life. I went to Gilmour Academy for High School and was a four year varsity ice hockey and lacrosse player. During my freetime, I enjoy hanging out with my friends and cooking. I would always help my mom and sister cook dinner for our family.

A little secret about me is that I love to travel. I have travelled the world with my family and one of my favorite things to do is to experiment with foods from different cultures. This is one of my favorite spots in Israel where we were able to take in the beautiful views of Tel Aviv while enjoying delicious appetizers and treats. Sometimes, it's the little things in life that make you happy.

Life is a beach and the beach certainly is better with friends! The Tel Aviv beach is the ultimate summer destination and I tried not to miss a day, especially after a long day of work! And what could be better than picnicking with a group of friends while watching the spectacular sunset over the Mediterranean Sea.

This is my family at my sister's graduation. We are extremely close and we love spending time together. I have 3 siblings, two of them are older and one of them is younger.
